Pork Sisig Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork belly, boiled and chopped into small cubes
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons calamansi juice (or lemon juice)
  • 1-2 red chili peppers, chopped (adjust to your preferred spice level)
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 egg (optional, for topping)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, heat the cooking oil and sauté the chopped onions until they turn translucent.
  2. Add the boiled pork belly cubes and cook for about 5-7 minutes until crispy edges form.
  3. Season the pork with soy sauce, vinegar, calamansi juice, chili peppers, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  4. Remove from heat and transfer to a sizzling plate (or a regular plate if you don’t have one).
  5. Top with a spoonful of mayonnaise and mix well to coat the pork. If desired, crack an egg on top and let it cook in the residual heat of the sizzling pork.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice or as a side dish.
